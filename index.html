Welcome to the stat calculator!

The stat calculator is a handy little tool to calculate stat growth. Your character's stats are displayed in the first column while the second column contains your "scale parameters". Scale Parameters are used in the formula to determine how your growth in a particular stat is impacted. In the second table, you can adjust the base values and the divisor value of the formula. In the last table you can see how each level up powers your character up and see the specific formula that is impacting each stat.
